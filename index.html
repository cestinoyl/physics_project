🔋 Capacitors

🧴 Electrolytic Capacitors

Description: Polarized capacitors with high capacitance. Used for power supply filtering and smoothing.

Values: 1µF – 10,000µF | Voltage: 6.3V – 450V | Polarity: Observe + and -

⚪ Ceramic Capacitors

Description: Small, non-polarized capacitors for high-frequency decoupling. Disc or MLCC type.

Values: pF to µF | Voltage: 50V – 1kV+

🎞️ Film Capacitors

Description: Non-polarized, stable, low-loss capacitors using plastic film dielectric.

Types: Polyester, Polypropylene | Values: 1nF – 10µF

📀 Polyester Film Capacitors

Description: Type of film capacitor using polyester as dielectric. Also called Mylar capacitors.

Values: 1nF – 10µF | Voltage: 50V – 630V | Tolerance: ±5% to ±20%

💎 Semiconductors

💡 LED Light Boards

Description: Pre-assembled boards with multiple LEDs for lighting applications.

Voltage: 3V-12V | Uses: Indicators, lighting, displays

💡 LED Components

Description: Individual LEDs for indicators, lighting, and displays.

Voltage: 1.8V-3.3V | Current: 20mA | Colors: Red, Green, Blue, Yellow, White

🔌 TO-92 Transistor

Description: Small plastic package transistor (BC547, 2N2222, etc.) for low-power switching/amplification.

Types: NPN or PNP | Uses: Signal amplification, small switches

🔌 TO-220 Transistors

Description: Medium-power transistors/mosfets in TO-220 package (IRFZ44N, TIP120, etc.).

Uses: Motor drivers, power supplies, high-current switching

🔌 SOT-23 Transistors

Description: Surface-mount transistors in tiny SOT-23 package for compact PCBs.

Uses: Modern electronics, phones, IoT devices

🔘 Integrated Circuit Chip

Description: Integrated circuit containing thousands to millions of electronic components.

Types: Logic ICs, op-amps, microcontrollers | Packages: DIP, SOP, QFP

🔧 Passives & Magnetics

🌀 Toroidal Inductor

Description: Wire wound on donut-shaped magnetic core. High efficiency, low EMI.

Uses: Power supplies, filters, audio crossovers | Inductance: µH to mH

🌀 Inductor Coil

Description: Wire wound into a coil that stores energy in a magnetic field.

Inductance: µH to mH | Uses: Filters, oscillators, power supplies

⚙️ SMPS Transformer

Description: High-frequency transformer used in switch-mode power supplies.

Uses: Phone chargers, computer PSUs, LED drivers

🔧 Axial Resistor

Description: Through-hole resistor with color-coded bands indicating resistance value.

Common values: 100Ω, 1kΩ, 10kΩ | Power rating: ¼W, ½W, 1W

🔔 Jumper Wire

Description: Flexible wire used to make connections on breadboards or between components.

Types: Male-male, male-female, female-female | Lengths: 10cm-30cm

🧊 Peltier Module

Description: Thermoelectric device that creates heat/cold when powered. Can also generate power from temperature difference.

Voltage: 12V | Uses: Cooling, heating, power generation

⚙️ Motors & Actuators

🔄 Brushless DC Motor

Description: High-efficiency motor without brushes, controlled by ESC.

Voltage: 12V-24V | Uses: Drones, RC vehicles, fans

📳 Vibration DC Motor

Description: Small motor with unbalanced weight that creates vibration when spinning.

Uses: Phones, game controllers, haptic feedback devices

❄️ Aluminum Heat Sink

Description: Finned metal piece that dissipates heat from power components.

Material: Aluminum | Mounting: Thermal paste + clip or screw

📡 Modules & Sensors

🔊 Audio Decoder Board

Description: PCB that decodes audio formats (MP3, WAV) for playback.

Uses: MP3 players, sound systems, DIY audio projects

🌫️ MQ Gas Sensor Module

Description: Gas detection module (MQ-2, MQ-7, MQ-135) for various gases.

Detection: LPG, CO, smoke, methane, alcohol | Output: Analog + Digital

📡 IR Sensor Module

Description: Detects infrared light for obstacle avoidance or remote control.

Range: 2cm-30cm | Output: Digital (obstacle yes/no)

🔊 Piezo Buzzer

Description: Converts electrical signal into sound using piezoelectric effect.

Types: Active (DC voltage = beep) or Passive (needs frequency signal)

🔊 Small Speaker

Description: Electrodynamic speaker that converts electrical signals to sound.

Impedance: 4Ω, 8Ω | Uses: Audio output, alarms, voice

📡 RF Receiver Module

Description: Wireless receiver for RF signals (315MHz, 433MHz, 2.4GHz).

Uses: Remote control, telemetry, wireless data

⚙️ Motor Driver Module

Description: PCB that controls motor speed and direction (L298N, L293D, etc.).

Voltage: 5V-12V | Current: 1A-2A per channel

🔌 Switches & Connectors

🔘 Slide Switch

Description: Sliding mechanism switch for ON/OFF or selector functions.

Configurations: SPST, SPDT, DPDT | Uses: Power switches, mode select

🔘 DIP Switch

Description: Small manual switch array for configuration settings.

Poles: 2-8 switches | Uses: Device configuration, address selection

🔘 Rotary Selector Switch

Description: Rotating knob switch that selects between multiple positions.

Positions: 2-12 | Uses: Mode selection, volume control, multi-function devices

🔘 Rocker Switch

Description: Pivoting switch that rocks back and forth (ON/OFF).

Uses: Power supplies, appliances, panel mounting

🔌 USB Type-A Port

Description: Standard rectangular USB connector for data and power.

Pins: VBUS (5V), D-, D+, GND | Current: Up to 2.4A

🎥 RCA AV Jack

Description: Phono connector for analog audio (red/white) and composite video (yellow).

Uses: AV equipment, vintage consoles, audio systems

🔌 XT60 Power Adapter

Description: High-current connector for RC and drone batteries.

Current rating: Up to 60A continuous | Uses: LiPo batteries, drones, RC cars

🔌 Male Pin Header

Description: Array of male pins for PCB connections (breadboard friendly).

Pitch: 2.54mm (0.1") | Configurations: 1xN, 2xN | Uses: Breakout boards, Arduino shields

🔋 Power & Miscellaneous

🔋 9V Battery

Description: Rectangular 9-volt battery with snap connector.

Type: Alkaline or rechargeable | Capacity: ~500mAh

🔋 Lithium-Ion Battery

Description: Rechargeable battery (18650, pouch, etc.) for portable electronics.

Nominal voltage: 3.7V | Full charge: 4.2V | Capacity: 500mAh-3500mAh

🔧 Charging Module PCB

Description: PCB for charging lithium batteries (TP4056, etc.) with protection.

Input: 5V USB | Charge current: 500mA-1A | Protection: Overcharge, overdischarge
